Description

Below is a short overview of our short-term, volatility-focused trading approach:

  • High Volatility Focus: We seek options with higher implied volatility for rapid price swings.
  • Short Holding Period: Positions typically last from a few hours to a few days.
  • Stop-Loss & Target: We aim for 10% risk and at least 2.5× potential reward.
  • Overnight Holds: Reserved for “Favorable” setups. Riskier trades often exit same day.
  • Metric-Based Decisions: Delta, Theta, IV, ATR, and Open Interest guide our entries.
  • Review & Refine: Tracking results helps improve over time and control emotions.
